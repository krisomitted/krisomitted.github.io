Bad Dream /
Follow your mother's voice towards the growing light.

DREAMER

  • LVL 21
  • HP: 60/100

You take a deep breath, calming yourself. Keeping your eyes closed, as if to protect yourself, you reply. "Death... Sleep... Sleep is the cousin of death, so that's who you are. You're Death." You brace yourself, every muscle in your body going tense, ready for another bout against the Voice. But instead you hear its laughter, clearly amused. The Voice quiets down and you feel the heaviness in your body dissapate. You're confused, and as if to counter that the Voice quips back. "You did well, little Dreamer. Just for that, I'll let you go. But this isn't over yet. Far from it." You become bewildered at its words, but before you can question it, the area around you begins to brighten with a soft light and you hear your mother calling from afar*.

*Your mother's voice is muffled, but you make out bits and pieces. It sounds a bit like: "Wake...... going...... be late.... break.....fas..... Shower....""
