Bad Dream /
Silence is all you will hear from now on.

DREAMER

  • LVL 21
  • HP: 0/100

"Oh, that's too bad then." You can hear the sneer in its voice. Before you can manage to speak up, you feel your body go limp, hitting the ground heavily. Without being able to move, you see the figure close in on you, even in the pitch black darkness it becomes visible. You feel your blood grow cold as the face of the darkness greets you, you close your eyes and wait. The Voice laughs condescendingly, "You are mine now." And all you hear after is silence.
